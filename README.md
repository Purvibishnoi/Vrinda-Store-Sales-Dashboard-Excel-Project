# Vrinda Store Sales Dashboard (Excel Project)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes Vrinda Store’s 2022 sales data to identify customer behavior, sales trends, product performance, and channel effectiveness. The objective is to transform raw data into meaningful insights through an interactive Excel dashboard to support data-driven decision-making.

## Steps Performed

* Data Cleaning
* Data Processing
* Data Analysis using Pivot Tables
* Data Visualization using Excel Dashboard

## Analysis and Insights

### Age and Gender Distribution

Adult women (30–49 years) contributed the highest number of orders at 34.59%, while senior men contributed the least at 5.91%.

### Gender Contribution

Women account for 64% of total sales, while men contribute 36%, making women the dominant customer segment.

### Sales by Channel

Amazon contributes the highest share of sales at 35.5%. Flipkart and Myntra are also major contributors, together forming a large portion of total sales.

### Regional Performance

Maharashtra is the top-performing state with sales of 2.99M, followed by Karnataka and Uttar Pradesh. These three states contribute approximately 25% of total sales.

### Order Status

92% of orders are successfully delivered. Returns (3%), cancellations (3%), and refunds (2%) remain low, indicating efficient operations.

### Monthly Trends

Sales and orders peak in March, showing a strong relationship between order volume and revenue generation.

## Dashboard Features

* Interactive slicers for Category, Month, and Channel
* Dynamic charts that update based on selected filters
* Clear visualization of trends and performance metrics

## Key Insights Summary

* Women contribute approximately 65% of total sales
* The 30–49 age group is the highest revenue-generating segment
* Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh are the top-performing states
* Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra contribute the majority of sales
* Most orders (92%) are successfully delivered

## Business Recommendations

* Focus marketing efforts on women aged 30–49
* Prioritize high-performing states such as Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh
* Strengthen presence on leading e-commerce platforms like Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra
* Implement targeted campaigns during peak periods, especially in March
